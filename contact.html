FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance for small events, and 6–8 weeks for large weddings or corporate gatherings to ensure ingredient sourcing and staffing.

Can I customize the menu for dietary restrictions? +

Absolutely! We cater to all dietary preferences — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, Jain, and more. Just mention your requirements while booking.

Do you provide setup and cleanup services? +

Yes! All packages include full setup and cleanup. Our team arrives 2–3 hours before the event to set up everything and stays until the event concludes.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Cancellations made 14+ days before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7–14 days incur a 25% fee. Within 7 days, 50% of the booking amount is retained.
